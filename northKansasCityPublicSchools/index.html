North Kansas City Public Schools - Attendance Zones

You can use this tool to view which school is available to you based on where you live.

1. Choose a school scenario, then type your address in the window at the top - a dropdown will appear to help you complete your address.



2. Press enter or click on the address in the window that appears to view the school attendance zone you are located in based on the school scenario that you have chosen.
